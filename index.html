About This Project

This personal website presents the results of my research on the genetic origins of the population of Msaken, Tunisia, through the study of Y-DNA (notably haplogroups J-L192 and R1a1 found among the Keramsa family), mtDNA haplogroups identified in local maternal lineages, and autosomal DNA comparisons with surrounding populations.
