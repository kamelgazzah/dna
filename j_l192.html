About Haplogroup J-L192

Haplogroup J-L192 is a subclade of haplogroup J, part of the larger Y-DNA haplogroup tree. It is primarily found in populations of the Middle East, particularly in Msaken, Tunisia (via J-L271), and in parts of Europe.
